[Migrated] Add 'Department of Education, Northern Ireland' and 'Education Scotland'

Standard Management Non-Core Codelist Amendments
rory_scott (Rory Scott) October 21, 2016, 1:42pm #1

As suggested in a pull request by Edafe Onerhime:

Proposed code: GB-IRN

  • Title: Department of Education, Northern Ireland
  • Description:
    The Department of Education, Northern Ireland issues a unique IRN
    (Institution Reference Number) to schools in Northern Ireland. The
    registry can be searched at http://apps.education-ni.gov.uk/appinstitutes/instmain.aspx and includes contact details and statistics for the schools.
  • Category: GB
  • URL: http://apps.education-ni.gov.uk/appinstitutes/instmain.asp
  • Public database: Yes

Proposed code: GB-SEED

  • Title: Education Scotland
  • Description: Education Scotland issues a unique seven-digit number to every Scottish school: http://www.educationscotland.gov.uk/parentzone/myschool/glossary.as… - The registry can be searched at http://www.educationscotland.gov.uk/parentzone/myschool/findaschool… and includes contact details of the schools.
  • Category: GB
  • URL: http://www.educationscotland.gov.uk/parentzone/myschool/findaschool…
  • Public database: Yes

dalepotter (Dale Potter) March 24, 2017, 11:54am #3

Just to flag that there appear to be issues with 3 of the 4 unique links in the original proposal.

  • The URL link for the Department of Education, Northern Ireland appears invalid, although appending ‘x’ does work - i.e. http://apps.education-ni.gov.uk/appinstitutes/instmain.asp -> http://apps.education-ni.gov.uk/appinstitutes/instmain.aspx

  • All links to http://www.educationscotland.gov.uk pages show a timeout. It appears this site has been replaced with the https://education.gov.scot domain.
    In terms of a searchable public database, it appears that the https://education.gov.scot/parentzone/find-a-school page has functionality to search by school name rather than SEED number.
