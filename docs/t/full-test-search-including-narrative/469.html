Matt (Matt Bartlett) May 18, 2016, 11:53am #4

It shows the results in the same format as the rest of d-portal, so first there’s a box showing all the activities found (according to your search) by publisher, then next a box showing the same results but by sector (although clicking on that isn’t working currently, so will fix that!).
The next section down is ‘Currently published to IATI’ where you could click on ‘Active projects’ (of which I get 11 for searching ‘World Humanitarian Summit’) to get a list of all activities related to your search that like this http://d-portal.org/ctrack.html?search=world%20humanitarian%20summit#view=active - which you can then explore further.
Is that more what you were hoping for?
Thanks
Matt
