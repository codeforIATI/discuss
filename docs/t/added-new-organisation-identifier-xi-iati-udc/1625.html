[Added] New Organisation Identifier: XI-IATI-UDC

Standard Management Non-Core Codelist Amendments
IATI-techteam (IATI Technical Team) February 21, 2019, 2:09pm #1

The following addition to the IATI Organisation identifier list has been proposed for United Darfur Committees. The request was reviewed by org-id.guide for the most appropriate identifier, and it was concluded that no alternative organisation identifier is currently available.

Code: XI-IATI-UDC
Name: United Darfur Committees
Description: United Darfur Committees is a non-governmental and non-profit organization established by committees of seven villages in East Dafur State, Sudan, to solve community conflict and assist the people in need.
URL: https://twitter.com/UnitedDarfur

If no objection is received this XI-IATI code will be approved and added in a week.
