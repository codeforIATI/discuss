Register for the IATI SDG Publishing Webinars- 30 October & 6 November

Notifications
IATI-techteam (IATI Technical Team) October 11, 2019, 9:12am #1

In September IATI published guidance on how to use the IATI Standard for publishing and using data on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The guidance is targeted at publishers who already have a knowledge of IATI and are looking to increase the usability of their data. It provides instructions on how to align activities with the SDG Goals, Targets and Indicators.

We’re holding two webinars to take publishers through the guidance, explain in more detail how to add SDG data into their IATI files, and answer any questions. The webinars will focus on:

  1. Demonstrating how to report SDG Goals & Targets using the element.

  2. Demonstrating how to report SDG Indicators as part of the element.

Participants should read the IATI SDG Guidance: Publishing SDG data using the IATI Standard in advance. It would also be helpful to send in questions ahead of the webinar via the Zoom registration links below or by emailing support@iatistandard.org.

Registration for the webinars is now open:

30 October 2019 3.30pm (UK) - Goals & Targets

Register here: https://zoom.us/meeting/register/c2e0e15b4d085ede8c34be5db4a05ad8

06 November 2019 3.30pm (UK) - Indicators

Register here: https://zoom.us/meeting/register/dd4bb8c4bf0b2f9c7510d14dfea9e911

If you are unable to attend these sessions, we will make recordings available.

Please note that these webinars will focus upon publishing SDG data in IATI. We are hoping to hold further webinars around accessing and using IATI SDG data next year. If you are interested in being involved in these webinars, please let us know by emailing us at: support@iatistandard.org.
