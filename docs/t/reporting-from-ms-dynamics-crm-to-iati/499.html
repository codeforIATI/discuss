Reporting from MS Dynamics CRM to IATI

Community Zone
Anneleen_BD (Anneleen Selleslach) June 29, 2016, 1:47pm #1

Broederlijk Delen, a Belgian NGO, is setting up an Aid Information Management System for the administrative and financial monitoring of programmes, projects and partner organisations in the Global South and to organise information regarding Monitoring and Evaluation. One of the goals for this system is to facilitate the publication of IATI data.

We have chosen Microsoft Dynamics CRM to build our Aid Information Management System. We plan to work via CSV to IATI for the publication of IATI data.

Are their organisations in this discussion forum that are already using Microsoft Dynamics CRM to report their IATI data? We are interested in exchanging best practices.
