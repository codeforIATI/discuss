Hi everyone,

At Partos we started with our IATI training programme in may 2013 with a support programme for Dutch NGO’s. As we’ve learned a lot about a structured approach to IATI publication I created this posts to share what we’ve learned and the training materials we are using. Central in our approach is a focus on high quality data sets and the practical uses of IATI data for individual organisations. For this support we have developed and are developing tools that help organisation structure their IATI process. Big thanks to Rolf Kleef and Pelle Aardema from Open for Change with whom we created these tools.

We started with enhancing the implementation schedule so it can serve as a step-by step guide to IATI implementation. The renewed implementation schedule helps organisation to structure their IATI implementation proces along four main steps. Starting to publish, planning to publish, data mapping and getting ready to publish. The Partos training programme is structured along the lines of these steps.

Implementation schedule download

Starting to publish
During the starting to publish seminar we help organisations in their first steps towards IATI publication. In this session we organise workshops around getting to know international stakeholders. We do this through customer journeys and persona templates.

Training materials :

  • Facilitator schedule (timetable)
  • Presentation from zero to IATI in 15(ish) minutes (Dutch)
  • Presentation Pelle Aardema, 7 uses of IATI data
  • Customer Journey
  • Persona Template

Planning to publish
The planning to publish session is meant to help organisations with their roadmap towards IATI, including the licenses, exclusion and external partners that need to be involved.

Training materials:

  • Presentation Rolf Kleef, Introduction & planning (odp)
  • Presentation Pelle Aardema, Planning publication, creating a roadmap
  • Presentation Rolf, Exclusions & licenses (odp)
  • Example planning template (courtesy of Red Cross Netherlands)
  • Empty planning template
  • Cordaid Example data use in communication folder
  • Exclusion poster exercise template
  • How to set up your prototype session
  • Simplified overview of IATI fields & Prototyping template
  • Facilitator schedule (timetable)

Data Mapping
Data Mapping is focused on helping organisations link their organisational information to the IATI fields. Central in our approach is to create a prototype data set in Aidstream through which the challenges and road towards a high qualitty IATI data set.
