[Added] to org-id (was Organisation Registration Agency) for Jordan Company Control Department

Standard Management Non-Core Codelist Amendments
TimDavies (Tim Davies) September 20, 2017, 2:29pm #1

Following a request from an IATI publisher this addition to the org-id.guide codelist has been staged.

Issue: https://github.com/org-id/register/pull/62

List title: Companies Control Department (Jordan)

Description: The Companies Control Department is an independent national financial and administrative institution affiliated to the Minister of Industry and Trade in Jordan under the provisions of the amended Companies Law No. (40) of 2002. The work of the department is governed by the 1997 Companies Law No.

The department is responsible for registration of various types of companies within the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, including non-profit companies.

It maintains a number of online services for searching company information.

Search URL: http://www.ccd.gov.jo/e-services/home/db

Preview this list at http://org-id.guide/_preview_branch/JO-CCD (visiting http://org-id.guide/list/JO-CCD when the preview is active or check the files changed options above.

Unless objections are raised, this update will be added to org-id.guide after 7 days.
