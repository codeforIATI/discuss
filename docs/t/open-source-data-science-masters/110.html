Open Source Data Science Masters

JohnAdams (John Adams) July 12, 2016, 1:43pm #1

@bjelkeman tweeted about this yesterday.

Thomas Bjelkeman @bjelkeman · Oct 12
If I was just about to go to university, I’d go to the uni that allowed me to study this: #datascience http://datasciencemasters.org/

The Open-Source Data Science Masters is an open-source curriculum for learning Data Science, built up from open and free resources. The topics covered are highly relevant to IATI and to the wider post-2015 Data Revolution. These are the skills the development community needs right now, and will increasingly need in the future.

Would people in the IATI community be interested in forming a virtual learning group to work through the curriculum together and support/mentor each other? I’m sure we would all benefit from a community supporting our continued professional development.

If you’re interested, please indicate in the comments below. Please contribute any suggestions on the next steps and how we might actually run such a community.
