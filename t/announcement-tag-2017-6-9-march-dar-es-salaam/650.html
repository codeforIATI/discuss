Announcement! TAG 2017, 6-9 March, Dar es Salaam

Events IATI TAG 2017
JoniHillman (Joni Hillman) December 22, 2016, 4:56pm #1

We are delighted to announce that the 2017 TAG meeting will be held in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania on 6-9 March 2017.

How to register for the TAG

  • Complete the registration form by midnight (GMT) on 18 January 2017.
  • By 20 January 2017 you’ll receive an email confirming whether you have secured a place, as well as information on the venue and accommodation options etc.
