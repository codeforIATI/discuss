CSO Working Group meeting 28 Jan 2014, Montreal

Community Zone CSOs
Wendy (Wendy Rogers) August 1, 2014, 10:32am #1

The first 2014 meeting of the IATI CSO Working Group meeting was held on Tuesday 28 Jan 2014 in the 4th floor meeting room at Development and Peace, 1425 René-Lévesque Blvd West, 3rd Floor Montréal QC H3G 1T7. The agenda can be downloaded below. Updates from members of the CSO Working Group, and the notes for this meeting can be found at:

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1SPzKvlNJK5xPqSSszOZr00EDgqlWlCO…

There was also a session on CSO Guidance at the TAG 2014 at 11:45am Weds 29 Jan 2014. The notes can be found at: http://www.aidtransparency.net/governance/tag/tag-meeting-2014/tag-… and then chose the right session.
